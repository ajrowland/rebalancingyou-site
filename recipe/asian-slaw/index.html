Asian Slaw

Who knew you could whip this up in pretty much under 5 minutes? It's a flavour sensation you will love and once you've made it you won't be able to stop! Serve with baked salmon, chicken satay or as a side to any red or green Thai curry. The perfect way to increase your daily vegetable intake!

◂ Back

Ingredients

  • 2 medium carrots
  • ½ a small red cabbage
  • A handful of mange tout
  • 2-3 spring onions
  • ½ a small red chilli
  • 1 tbsp mixed sesame seeds
  • A handful chopped fresh coriander
  • For the dressing
  • 2 tbsp tamari or gluten free soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp sesame oil
  • 1 tbsp rice vinegar
  • 1 tsp honey
  • 1 clove garlic crushed

Method

  1. Peel and grate the carrot and place in a bowl, finely slice the red cabbage, spring onion and mange tout add these to the carrot and mix well.
  2. Chop the red chilli and add desired amount to the prepared vegetables
  3. Mix all the dressing ingredients together, pour over the mix well.
  4. Scatter over the coriander and sesame seeds.

Notes

This dressing works really well poured over warm roasted aubergine and courgette as a side to fish or chicken.
