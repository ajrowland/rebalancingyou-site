Creamy Smoked Mackerel Pate

Mackerel is a good source of protein and is high in omega three. Serve with vegetable sticks to count towards your five a day.

◂ Back

Ingredients

  • 200 g smoked peppered mackerel
  • 50g light cream cheese
  • 1 tbsp natural yogurt
  • Zest and juice of half a lemon
  • 1 tbsp chopped herbs (such as parsley or chives)
  • 1 tbsp olive oil and extra for drizzling.
  • 1 heaped tsp creamed horseradish
  • 1 spring onion (green part)

Method

  1. Roughly chop the spring onion and peel the skin from the mackerel.
  2. Use the small side of a grater to remove the zest from the lemon and add it, and the rest of the ingredients to a food processor, squeezing in the lemon juice last. Turn the processor on and mix for 3-4 minutes. Taste and adjust seasoning if needed.
  3. Place in a serving bowl and drizzle with a little extra virgin olive oil before serving.
  4. Serve with crusty bread or vegetable sticks.

Tasty alternatives

Add a few capers.

Use a Greek yogurt for an extra creamy taste
